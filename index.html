What's New

Phase 3 — AI & Plans · July 2026

  • Plan nudges — set a weekly session target on your plan and get a nudge when the week is slipping; plans without a target nudge after a few idle days
  • PR celebration — beating your all-time best on any exercise now gets the moment it deserves
  • Home screen dashboard — week view of training days, week streak, and a hook from your last session (beat your best!)
  • Quick log — one tap logs the same weight and reps as last session; repeats your last set when you go beyond it
  • Exercise history — tap History on the home screen to see a progression chart, stats, and session log for any exercise
  • Workout plans — create named training blocks with exercises, target sets×reps, duration, and objectives
  • Active plan guides your session: exercise picker shows plan exercises first with targets; Up Next follows plan order
  • Plan adherence shown on the completed screen; expiry nudge when duration is up
  • AI session summary — tap "AI Summary" after finishing to get a natural language breakdown powered by Claude
  • AI summary includes plan context: week number, objectives, and what was completed vs skipped

Phase 2.1 — Usability · May 2026

  • Exercise picker sorted by most recently used; A–Z toggle available
  • Export workout history as date-filtered CSV from Settings
  • Clear All Data de-emphasised to avoid accidental taps
  • Confirm before discarding an incomplete session

Phase 2 — Habit & Progression · May 2026

  • In-session progression signal — highlights personal bests and improvements
  • Smart session reminder — nudge shown at your usual training time
  • Up Next hint — guides you through your previous session's exercise order
  • Session completion summary — volume and top improvement on finish
  • Weight unit (kg/lbs) stored per set for accurate cross-session comparisons
  • Rest timer accurate after phone lock or background

Phase 1 — Core Logging · April 2026

  • Log sets with weight & reps, or duration & calories for cardio
  • Previous session performance shown as placeholder in inputs
  • Elapsed session timer; resume within 60 minutes of finishing
  • Delete individual sets; undo last set
  • Rest timer between sets (90 seconds)
  • Custom "Other" exercise with automatic cardio detection
  • Auto-save to Google Drive on session finish
  • Inactivity timeout after 30 minutes
  • Session notes on the completed screen
  • Settings: weight unit, clear all data
